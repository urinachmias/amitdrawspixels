Our Privacy Commitment

Amit Draws Pixels does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. This is a privacy-focused app designed to work entirely on your device.

Information Collection and Use

Information We DO NOT Collect

  • No Personal Information: We do not collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, or any other personal identifiers
  • No Usage Data: We do not track how you use the app
  • No Analytics: We do not use any analytics services
  • No Advertising Identifiers: We do not collect or use advertising IDs
  • No Location Data: We do not access or collect your location
  • No Device Information: We do not collect device IDs, IP addresses, or system information
  • No Cookies or Tracking Technologies: We do not use cookies, pixels, beacons, or any tracking technologies

Information Stored Locally

All your drawings and app data are stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. This data:

  • Never leaves your device unless you explicitly share it
  • Is under your complete control
  • Can be deleted by uninstalling the app

Optional Tip Jar

The app includes an optional tip jar feature powered by Apple's In-App Purchase system. When you make a tip:

  • The transaction is processed entirely by Apple
  • We receive only a notification that a tip was made
  • No personal information is shared with us
  • Apple's Privacy Policy governs their handling of payment information
  • You can review your purchase history in your Apple account

Third-Party Services

The only third-party service used is Apple's StoreKit for processing optional tips. No other third-party services, SDKs, or libraries that could collect data are integrated into the app.

Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not share, sell, rent, or trade any information because we do not collect any information.

Data Security

Since we don't collect any data, there is no data to secure on our servers. Your drawings are secured by iOS's built-in security features on your device.

Children's Privacy

Amit Draws Pixels does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under 13. The app is safe for users of all ages.

International Users

This app is developed in Israel and available worldwide. Since no data is collected or transmitted, there are no international data transfer concerns.

Your California Privacy Rights

CCPA/CPRA Rights

Under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) and California Privacy Rights Act (CPRA), California residents have specific rights regarding their personal information. Since we do not collect any personal information:

  • Right to Know: There is no personal information to disclose
  • Right to Delete: No deletion necessary as we don't collect data
  • Right to Opt-Out: No opt-out needed as we don't sell personal information
  • Right to Non-Discrimination: All users are treated equally
  • Right to Correct: No data to correct
  • Right to Limit Use: No sensitive personal information is used

CalOPPA Compliance

In compliance with the California Online Privacy Protection Act (CalOPPA):

  • Privacy Policy Accessibility: This privacy policy is easily accessible from the app and any app store listings
  • Policy Updates: Any changes will be reflected with an updated "Last Updated" date
  • Personal Information Disclosure: We do not collect any personally identifiable information
  • Do Not Track Signals: The app does not track users, so Do Not Track signals are inherently respected
  • Third-Party Tracking: No third parties are permitted to collect personally identifiable information through our app

Your Choices

  • Using the App: You can use all features of the app without providing any personal information
  • Tipping: The tip jar is entirely optional and does not affect app functionality
  • Data Deletion: Delete all local data by uninstalling the app

Changes to This Privacy Policy
