Why Amit Draws Pixels?

Amit is my 9 year old daughter, she loves making pixel art. I wasn't able to find a good, free pixel art app for her so I created this one to be distributed freely.

The app is dedicated to her and the artworks in the app store images are her creations. Every feature was designed with kids like Amit in mind - simple enough to start creating right away, but powerful enough to make something amazing.

— Uri Nachmias, Developer & Dad
